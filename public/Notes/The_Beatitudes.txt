The Beatitudes
wiki - https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Beatitudes

[0] Blessed are the poor in spirit,
for theirs is the kingdom of heaven.

[1] Blessed are they who mourn,
for they will be comforted.

[2] Blessed are the meek,
for they will inherit the land.

[3] Blessed are they who hunger and thirst for righteousness,
for they will be satisfied.

[4] Blessed are the merciful,
for they will be shown mercy.

[5] Blessed are the clean in heart,
for they will see God.

[6] Blessed are the peacemakers,
for they will be called children of God.

[7] Blessed are they who are persecuted for the sake of righteousness,
for theirs is the kingdom of heaven.

[8] Blessed are you when people insult you, persecute you and falsely say all kinds of evil against you because of Me.
